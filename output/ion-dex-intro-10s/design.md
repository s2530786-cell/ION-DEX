# ION DEX — Video design (10s intro)

## Mood
Premium Web3 trading launch — cyberpunk neon, confident, fast but not chaotic.

## Canvas
- Background: `#070b1a` → `#12082a` radial depth
- Accents: cyan `#22d3ee`, violet `#a78bfa`, magenta `#e879f9`, gold `#fbbf24`

## Typography
- Display: `Segoe UI`, system-ui, sans-serif (fallback; no custom woff2 bundled)
- Headline weight 800, label weight 600, mono metadata 500

## Do
- Glass panels, neon rims, aurora glows, starfield texture
- Large type (64–120px headlines)

## Don't
- Flat grey UI, tiny 14px body, placeholder copy
