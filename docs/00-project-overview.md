# ION DEX Project Overview

ION DEX is planned as a full-stack Web3 trading and ecosystem portal for Ice Open Network.
It combines professional spot trading, AMM liquidity, staking, burn analytics, bridge flows,
ION DNS domains, ION Identity, AI market intelligence, and transparent treasury operations.

## Product Positioning

ION DEX is not only a swap interface. It is the trading, identity, domain, staking, bridge,
analytics, and asset-management entry point for the ION ecosystem.

## Core Product Modules

- Dashboard: market overview, swap entry, charts, TVL, APR, burn and staking summaries.
- Swap: market swap for ION pairs, including BNB/ION.
- Trade: professional K-line, limit orders, depth, fills, and account order history.
- Grid: on-chain spot grid strategies with keeper-based execution.
- Pool: add/remove liquidity, LP positions, fee revenue, and impermanent-loss hints.
- Stake: official staking, DEX staking, LP staking, and future ecosystem staking statistics.
- Burn: dual-chain burn tracking for BSC ION and ION mainnet ION.
- Bridge: ION Chain and BSC cross-chain transfers with status tracking.
- Domain: .ion domain search, resolve, profile, transfer, listing, and marketplace.
- Identity: ION ID / KYC Pass status, privacy-preserving credential checks, and badges.
- AI Market: on-chain AI analysis, whale tracking, risk scoring, and strategy suggestions.
- Treasury: fee distribution, community rewards, team allocation, staking rewards, and reserves.
- Profile: avatar, wallets, language, preferences, security, notifications, badges, and records.
- Admin: internal operations console for configs, token lists, risk switches, and announcements.
- Transparency: public contract addresses, fees, burn addresses, treasury, audits, and status.

- Ultra-thin animated neon gradient borders.

- Aurora background and rotating galaxy background, switched hourly.

- Responsive desktop, tablet, and mobile layouts.

## Non-Negotiable Engineering Principles

- User funds are always controlled by user wallets.
- Backend services never sign user asset transactions.
- Sensitive identity and KYC raw data must not be stored by ION DEX.
- Chain state is the source of truth; backend/indexer data is a cache and analytics layer.
- High-risk admin operations require multisig and timelock.
- AI output is advisory only and must not be represented as guaranteed investment advice.
- No production release without audits, testnet campaigns, monitoring, and incident playbooks.
